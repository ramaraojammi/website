About Me

I am Jammi Rama Rao, working as librarian in V. T. College, Rajamahendravaram since three decades.

Born at Rajamahendravaram on 9th February, 1961.

Beloved Parents: Jammi Narayana Rao and Satyavathi

Educational Qualifications: M.A (History), M.A (Political Science), M.L.I.Sc, B.Com, B.L.I.Sc

Spouse: "Sahitya Ratna" D.V.K.S.Lakshmi (Roopa), M.A (Economics), B.Ed

Son: Narayana Praneeth Jammi, M.Tech

Secretery, Veeresalingam Town Hall Trust Board, Rajamahendravaram

Hononary Librarian & Musium Director, India Independence Center, Freedom Park, Palchowk, Rajamahendravaram

Hononary Secretry, Andhra Kesari Centenary Junior College, Rajamahendravaram

Convenor, Andhra Kesari Balala Samithi, Rajamahendravaram
